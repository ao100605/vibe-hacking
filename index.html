What are the SDGs?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are 17 interconnected global goals adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015. They provide a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future. The best part? Everyone can contribute to achieving these goals through everyday actions!

No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere.

Social

What You Can Do:

  • Donate to reputable poverty relief organizations
  • Support local food banks and shelters
  • Buy from fair trade businesses
  • Volunteer your time and skills

Zero Hunger

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition.

Social

What You Can Do:

  • Reduce food waste at home
  • Support local farmers and food producers
  • Donate to food banks regularly
  • Compost organic waste
3

Good Health and Well-Being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all.

Social

What You Can Do:

  • Exercise regularly and maintain a balanced diet
  • Support mental health awareness
  • Get vaccinated and encourage others
  • Donate blood regularly
4

Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all.

Social

What You Can Do:

  • Tutor or mentor students in your community
  • Donate books and educational materials
  • Support literacy programs
  • Share your knowledge and skills freely
5

Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

Social

What You Can Do:

  • Challenge gender stereotypes and biases
  • Support women-owned businesses
  • Advocate for equal pay and opportunities
  • Educate yourself and others on gender issues
6

Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure access to water and sanitation for all.

Environmental

What You Can Do:

  • Conserve water in daily activities
  • Fix leaks promptly
  • Don't pollute water sources
  • Support organizations providing clean water
7

Affordable and Clean Energy

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable energy.

Environmental

What You Can Do:

  • Switch to renewable energy providers
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Turn off lights and unplug devices
  • Consider solar panels for your home
8

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive economic growth and decent work.

Economic

What You Can Do:

  • Support ethical businesses
  • Advocate for workers' rights
  • Buy products from companies with fair labor practices
  • Develop your skills through continuous learning

Economic

What You Can Do:

  • Support innovative and sustainable businesses
  • Use public transportation
  • Advocate for better local infrastructure

Reduced Inequalities

Social

What You Can Do:
